About


Moda Designs was founded in 1990, and thanks to the vision of the owners and the dedicated work of its employees, it soon became became a leading company in the field of interior design and furniture distribution. Due to the growing focus on project work, in mid-2015. business we moved from showroom to the office in which we operate today. We provide clients with consulting, design, equipment and execution services throughout Croatia and Bosnia and Herzegovina. Need just one piece of furniture? No problem, make an appointment and choose from a rich offer tables, chairs, chests of drawers, sofas, armchairs, closets, mirrors ..... if you need help choosing, we will be happy to help you give some advice too! If you are not able to reach us, or you are outside Zagreb, send an inquiry by e-mail and request offer. We represent numerous renowned manufacturers of furniture, lighting, accessories and small inventory, primarily from Italy, but also from other European countries. We also offer custom-made furniture. We can proudly say that we have equipped many private facilities as well as institutions, and the experience we have gathered in these 26 years of work, thanks to the trust of our clients, we are upgrading even today because every new project is also new challenge.
